About Our School

Learn who we are, what we stand for, and why families trust us.

A Legacy of
Learning & Growth

Founded in 1995, Apex Learning Hub has been a cornerstone of educational excellence in our community. We believe every child has unique potential — our mission is to discover and nurture it.

Our state-of-the-art facilities, dedicated faculty, and student-centred approach create an environment where curiosity thrives and achievements are celebrated at every stage.

From science labs to sports fields, arts studios to computer rooms — Greenwood equips students with the skills, values, and confidence to succeed in a rapidly changing world.
